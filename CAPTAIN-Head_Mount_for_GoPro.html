Head Mount for GoPro I CAPTAIN


The advantages of this type of mount:
+ Mount for GoPro "Captain" is attached to the head with an adjustable clasp and is comparable in convenience with a regular cap.
+ The mount is adjustable in size and equally comfortable for both men and women.
+ The mount is made of lightweight, flexible material and transfers evenly the weight of the camera around the entire circumference of the mount.
Advantages over the helmet for GoPro:
 - the head can breathe.
- much more convenient.
- more compact

You can already purchase the finished product on the website: http://shpws.me/RTGu

Attached files (3D model) in the format: stl, asm (Creo 4.0).
